Vikas Samiti Rankuna (VSR NGO)

VSR NGO is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable community development and social welfare in the Rankuna region. Established with a mission to uplift marginalized groups, we focus on areas such as education, healthcare, women's empowerment, and environmental conservation. Our projects are designed to address the unique needs of local communities, providing access to vital resources and opportunities for growth. Through collaborative efforts with volunteers, donors, and local partners, we have successfully impacted numerous lives, ensuring a brighter future for the underprivileged. At VSR NGO, we believe that real change begins with grassroots initiatives, and we are committed to fostering a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Join us in our mission to make a difference and build a better tomorrow for Rankuna and beyond.

Our Mission

At VSR NGO, we believe that every individual, regardless of their background, deserves equal opportunities for growth and prosperity. Our mission is to provide the tools and resources necessary to improve lives, strengthen communities, and promote self-reliance. By focusing on grassroots development, we aim to create a foundation for long-term, sustainable change.

Key Focus Areas

  • Education:We strive to improve access to quality education for children in rural and underserved communities, offering them the chance for a brighter future.
  • Healthcare:Our healthcare initiatives focus on providing medical assistance to low-income families, raising awareness about health and hygiene, and conducting health camps.
