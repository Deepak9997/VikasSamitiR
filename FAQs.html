Frequently Asked Questions

What is VSR NGO?

VSR NGO is a non-governmental organization focused on community development and social upliftment in Rankuna.

How can I volunteer?

You can sign up for volunteer opportunities through our website's volunteer page or contact us directly via email.

Where is VSR NGO located?

We are based in Rankuna. Our main office is located at 123 Community Road, Rankuna, India.

Who benefits from VSR’s programs?

VSR’s programs benefit underprivileged children, families, and individuals in need of education, healthcare, food, and livelihood support.

How can I apply for assistance from VSR?

You can apply for assistance by contacting us through our website or visiting our local offices for more information.

Does VSR operate only in specific areas?

VSR operates in various regions, prioritizing communities with the greatest need. We aim to expand our reach to more areas over time.

How can I stay updated on VSR’s activities?

You can follow us on social media, subscribe to our newsletter, or visit our website for regular updates on our activities and achievements.

How can I donate to support VSR NGO?

You can donate through our secure online portal, available on our donation page, or by directly contacting our office.
